Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Its name is frake - my personal brand. It offers online courses and coaching services about speaking/communication for English learners.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

IELTS English Speaking course: Vietnamese students, age ranging from 15-30, who wants to get a high score in the Speaking section of the IELTS exam.

Communication Coaching: 1-to-1 service, based on needs, anyone aged between 15-30.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

Students in Vietnam lack access to quality Speaking training and many struggle with Speaking skills when it comes to learning English. I want to provide a solution by sharing the knowledge and tips I've earned throught the experienced learner and an international student.

Same thing with Communication Coaching. A lot of Vietnamese young adults lack proper training in communicating, leading to missed opportunities for dream jobs or failed relationships with their loved ones. Meanwhile, there are not many coaching services in Vietnam, even though this type of service is popular in a lot of places in the world.
