About frake.io

Frake is a pioneering online education platform dedicated to empowering learners globally. With a commitment to excellence, we offer a diverse range of high-quality courses designed to inspire curiosity, foster growth, and unlock potential. From language proficiency to technical skills, our interactive and inclusive community ensures that everyone, regardless of background or circumstance, can access transformative learning experiences. Join us at Frake and embark on a journey of discovery, where education knows no bounds.

About frake • english Discord Server

frake • english's mission is to bring all Vietnamese youth together, helping them—and having them help each other—achieve a shared goal: break ineffective English learning conventions, and master English communication skills.

Attracted 3,000 members in 8 months, “frake • english” is:

  • a welcoming space that actively accentuates diversity, equity, and inclusion, where every voice is heard;
  • a digital playground that connects English learners from all levels to share wisdom and learn from each other;
  • a gathering hub of the most advanced yet humble English enthusiasts, be it certified IETLS trainers, university lecturers, language influencers, students from prestigious schools, or more.
